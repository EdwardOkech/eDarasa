How it works

  • Gain the skills employers want through our intensive 25-50 hrs online training and work project

    Course 1. Introduction to the Job Search
    Course 2. Basic Skills for the Job Search (CV & cover letter writing, Research & Networking
    Course 3. Impressing Employers: Thinking Skills
    Course 4: Impressing Employers: Doing Skills
    Course 5: Impressing Employers: Interviewing


  • When ready, attend & pass our in-person interview & verification process in Nairobi

    Stage 1: Personality Test
    Stage 2: Verbal and Numerical Reasoning
    Stage 3: Overall Interview
    Stage 4: Industry-specific Interview
    Stage 5: Project Presentation


  • Get listed on our database and begin receiving application-free interview invitations from employers in our network

    Feature 1: Specify your availability date
    Feature 2: Build and customize your profile page
    Feature 3: Receive instant text messages when interview invitations arrive
    Feature 4: We actively market you by showcasing you for up to 3 times in our bi-weekly newsletter to employers


  • Receive personalized support from Workstry during your employment

    Feature 1: Ask for advice on how to handle various aspects of your new job
    Feature 2: We actively seek out more advanced training for your by pitching your employers
    Feature 3: Access to Workstry's networking and career building events
