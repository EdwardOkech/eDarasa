Frequently asked questions

For jobseekers.

Q: How does Workstry work?

A:Workstry is an online training and recruitment program. Jobseekers interested in gaining job search and job skills register and pay a small fee to undergo our proprietary training on our site www.workstry.com. After successfully completing the training and passsing a face-to-face interview with workstry interviewers, these trainees are listed on our searchable database and also presented via email to our network of employers. They receive interview invitations from employers more often and more easily than non-trainees and are also more likely to pass their interviews and secure jobs/internships quicker.

Q: What kind of training do you offer?

A:Workstry’s job-seekers’ training comprises of five training areas and within each of these several modules:

  1. understanding the job search process
  2. basic skills for the job search
  3. thinking skills to impress employers
  4. skills in applying training
  5. and specialized courses in several high demand fields

Q: Am I eligible for Workstry training?

A:All job-seekers can benefit from taking our training and improving their job search and job related skills. However, Workstry’s training is specifically built for people who feel that their need guidance in navigating the job search process.

Q: Is your training just written materials that I read?

A: Our training is highly interactive and includes reading materials as well as in-lesson exercises. In a short while, we will also launch discussion forums to enable interact among trainees as well as with jobsmentor trainers; games and an interactive interview engine.

Q: I already have a degree/diploma/certificate, why do I need your training?

A:While your degree/diploma/certificate has given you academic understanding of your chosen field, it unfortunately has not prepared you for the rigors of job seeking. If a recent graduate, you are likely not to understand how employers hire; what you should and should not do; what opportunities exist for you etc. Workstry builds up all the other skills you need to transform your academic training into value for an employer and a job for yourself

Q: How long does the training take?

A: Workstry training should take at minimum 25 hours of study. It is likely to last longer. There is much to learn and rushing through the training does not necessarily mean getting hired faster. Your success in securing and passing interviews will depend to a large extent on how well you internalize the training. We advise, therefore, that you commit at least 50 hours of study and practice.

Q: How much do I have to pay to join Workstry?

A:Workstry charges a registration fee of Kes 1000. This fee is, however, refundable, minus MPESA fees, under the following conditions: 1) successful completion of the training and 2) failure to secure either a placement (job or internship) or five interview invitations with reputable employers through Workstry, in the three months after you successfully complete training and pass your face-to-face interview with a workstry interviewer.

Q: If I pay this registration fee do you give a guarantee I will get a job through your site?

A: Workstry does not give job guarantees. We improve and increase your chances of securing jobs/internships faster by providing you with comprehensive training and thereafter, opportunities to present yourself to employers. Your success in interviews solely depends on how well you utilize the training and is not under the control of Workstry.

Q: How do I make my registration fee payment?

A: The registration fee is payable through our MPESA paybill number. Instructions will be available once our platform launches on 21st January 2016.

Q: Does this registration fee cover everything?

A:This registration fee will allow you to undertake our entire job search training. The training, however, excludes our premium services offered at an extra pay per use fee.

Q: What other services do you offer and how much do they cost?

A:Among other services, we are soon to launch a premium service that will allow you to take mock interviews with professionals in your field and thus gain experience in interviewing prior to undertaking real interviews. This service will come at a cost separate from our registration fee.

Q: Which employers recruit from Workstry?

A: Currently we work with three market research companies, a digital marketing agency, an ICT skills training company, a green energy vendor, a software development agency and various companies interested in hiring for sales and business development. In coming months, we will add more employers to our list.

Q: How much will the job I get through Workstry pay me?

A: Workstry will NOT, at any point, be involved in salary negotiations between you and your future employer. We do not determine wages or advise employers on what to pay. Your pay from a job secured through Workstry will depend on your future employer’s considerations as well as your ability to negotiate a good package using the skills we provide you in training.

Q: Do you have any testimonials from people who have actually secured jobs through you?

A: These are on display on our homepage.

Q: By the way, are you Workstry or are you Jobsmentor?

A: Apologies about the confusion. Jobsmentor is the register company that owns the online platform Workstry. We have recently changed our operating name from Jobsmentor to Workstry.

Q: I have a question that is not covered here. How can I contact you?

A: Kindly click on “contact us” on the top menu bar. A form will appear in which you can fill in your details and type in a question. Afterwards click “submit” and we will contact you as soon as possible.
